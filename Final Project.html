The task at hand was to integrate 3d printing, Laser Cutting and electronic into our product.

We started out by looking at some ideas. Our original idea was to do a nixie clock.

We plan to change the base into 3d printing while lazer cutting for the panel. We would also need to programme the electrical for LED light. For our Lazercutting we planned onnusing a clear arcyic as to light pass through.

However due to the lack of time, we had to consideration the duration of make it would be too long and decided to change it into a moving light display.

We started by drawing out the size of the base and panels while learning more about how the electrical surges are calculated

Jin sheng and ye chun, I then drew it on fusion360 and Sketchup so we would have a better understanding.
